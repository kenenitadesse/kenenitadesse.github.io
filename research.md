## Current Projects

### Age-It: Ageing Well in an Ageing Society
A Spoke 4, WP5 project funded by the European Union - Next Generation EU.  
*Principal Investigator: Prof. Giovanna Boccuzzo*

This project aims to develop statistical models to understand and predict frailty indicators in the aging population, using large-scale survey and administrative data.

### Pharmacovigilance & Drug Safety
My PhD research focuses on developing statistical methods for post-market drug safety surveillance. This includes:
- Developing disproportionality methods for spontaneous reporting systems.
- Applying natural language processing (NLP) to electronic health records to detect adverse drug events.
- Analyzing real-world data for safety signal detection.

### GLASS Lab
As a member of [GLASS](https://www.glass-statistics.it/), I collaborate on research that applies statistical methods to social and health-related problems, promoting open science and reproducible research.
