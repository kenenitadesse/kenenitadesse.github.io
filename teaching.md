## Course Descriptions

### Statistical Theory & Inference
- **Stat3121 - Statistical Theory of Distributions:** Probability distributions, moment generating functions, transformations, and sampling distributions.
- **Stat3122 - Statistical Inference:** Point estimation, hypothesis testing, confidence intervals, and likelihood theory.

### Multivariate & Advanced Methods
- **Stat3133 - Introduction to Multivariate Methods:** Principal component analysis, factor analysis, discriminant analysis, and clustering techniques.
- **Stat3131 - Econometrics:** Linear regression models, panel data, time series econometrics, and model diagnostics.

### Biostatistics & Demography
- **Stat3101 - Biostatistics and Epidemiology:** Study designs, measures of association, logistic regression, and survival analysis applications in health research.
- **Stat3142 - Demography:** Population dynamics, fertility, mortality, migration, and demographic methods.

### Statistical Computing
- **Stat2081 - Statistical Computing I:** Introduction to SPSS for data management and analysis.
- **Stat2082 - Statistical Computing II:** Data manipulation, statistical modeling, and visualization using R.

### Core Statistics
- **Stat1011 - Basic Statistics:** Descriptive statistics, probability, sampling distributions, and introductory inference.
- **Stat1012 - Introduction to Probability Theory:** Probability axioms, conditional probability, random variables, and expectation.
- **Stat1013 - Statistical Methods:** t-tests, ANOVA, correlation, regression, and nonparametric methods.
- **Stat2091 - Sampling Theory:** Simple random sampling, stratified sampling, cluster sampling, and sample size determination.

### Applied Statistics
- **Stat2102 - Time Series Analysis:** ARIMA models, forecasting, stationarity, and seasonal decomposition.
- **Stat3172 - Social and Economic Statistics:** Application of statistical methods to social and economic data.

- Hands-on computing and data analysis
- Developing critical thinking about statistical methods
- Supporting students through clear explanations and examples
